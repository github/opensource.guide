Joint Interoperability of Tactical Command and Control Systems or JINTACCS is a United States military program for the development and maintenance of tactical information exchange configuration items  CIs  and operational procedures  It was originated to ensure that the command and control  C2 and C3  and weapons systems of all US military services and NATO forces would be interoperable   It is made up of standard Message Text Formats  MTF  for man readable and machine processable information  a core set of common warfighting symbols  and data link standards called Tactical Data Links  TDLs
