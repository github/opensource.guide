In the context of mailing lists  discussion groups  discussion forums  bulletin boards  newsgroups  and wikis a contribution is off topic if it is not within the bounds of the current discussion  and on topic if it is   Even on very specialized forums and lists off topic posting is not necessarily frowned upon  but a common netiquette convention is to mark a new off topic posting or email by beginning it with  OT    for example in a forum discussing the Linux operating system someone might post   OT  Wow  did you feel that earthquake     Posting off topic messages to deliberately aggravate other members is a form of trolling   1  See also  Netiquette  2  References  3  External links  ru Сетевой этикет#Оффтопик
